Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, use our services, or contact us for support. This may include:

  • Name, email address, and contact information
  • Project data and files you upload
  • Usage data and preferences
  • Financial data accessed through QuickBooks integration (with your explicit consent)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Integrate with third-party services like QuickBooks (with your permission)

3. QuickBooks Data Integration

When you connect your QuickBooks account:

  • We only access data you explicitly authorize during the connection process
  • We use this data solely to provide project management and financial integration features
  • We do not share your QuickBooks data with unauthorized third parties
  • You can disconnect the integration at any time
  • We comply with Intuit's security and privacy requirements

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except:

  • To provide services you've requested (like QuickBooks integration)
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure data storage practices

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. We may retain certain information after account closure for legal compliance or legitimate business purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Disconnect third-party integrations
  • Export your data

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience, analyze usage, and provide personalized content. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9. Third-Party Services

Our service may integrate with third-party services (like QuickBooks, Google services, etc.). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through our service.
